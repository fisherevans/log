A portable, rechargeable UV-charging box for glow discs - with built-in red night-vision lights and a shoulder strap. Originally posted to [/r/discgolf](https://www.reddit.com/r/discgolf/comments/r0izbb/i_built_a_rechargeable_glowdiscbox/).

![The box in all its glory!](https://media.fisher.sh/blog/2021/11/23/rechargeable-glow-disc-box/01.jpg)

The box in all its glory! The UV lights are normally off when the lid is open (to stop you from blinding yourself). The switch is taped down for this photo.

![UV LED strips between each disc](https://media.fisher.sh/blog/2021/11/23/rechargeable-glow-disc-box/02.jpg)

There are UV LED strips between each disc. I spray painted the inside silver to help reflect the UV light on all sides of the disc.

![Rechargeable battery + charge-level mirror](https://media.fisher.sh/blog/2021/11/23/rechargeable-glow-disc-box/03.jpg)

It's powered by a removable, 12v, 3000mAh, rechargeable battery. I added a 45-degree mirror so you can easily see the current charge level.

![Dimmable red LEDs on both sides](https://media.fisher.sh/blog/2021/11/23/rechargeable-glow-disc-box/04.jpg)

The red LEDs are dimmable and are on both sides. No more pulling out my phone flashlight to make sure I don't trip, and I get to keep those night eyes :)

![Controls panel](https://media.fisher.sh/blog/2021/11/23/rechargeable-glow-disc-box/05.jpg)

The left slider controls the UV brightness (if you're trying to conserve battery). The middle dial controls the red LEDs. The lid-switch is on the left, held down by a paintbrush.

![Shoulder strap from an old camera bag](https://media.fisher.sh/blog/2021/11/23/rechargeable-glow-disc-box/06.jpg)

I salvaged a shoulder strap from an old camera bag - it sits right on my hip, which is perfect. I was really happy with how comfortable it is to carry. (I'm 6'5" for reference.)

![Strap doesn't block the lid](https://media.fisher.sh/blog/2021/11/23/rechargeable-glow-disc-box/07.jpg)

I made sure that the straps wouldn't stop you from opening the box while wearing it.

![The original prototype](https://media.fisher.sh/blog/2021/11/23/rechargeable-glow-disc-box/08.jpg)

This was the original prototype I made months ago. The final product was only slightly larger.

![Prototype shoulder strap](https://media.fisher.sh/blog/2021/11/23/rechargeable-glow-disc-box/09.jpg)

Shoulder strap included! :D

![Prototype vs final, side by side](https://media.fisher.sh/blog/2021/11/23/rechargeable-glow-disc-box/10.jpg)

Here are both compared to each other.

![Prototype vs final, both open](https://media.fisher.sh/blog/2021/11/23/rechargeable-glow-disc-box/11.jpg)

And both open.

![Plexiglass sheets with UV LED strips](https://media.fisher.sh/blog/2021/11/23/rechargeable-glow-disc-box/12.jpg)

Between each disc are two plexiglass sheets with UV LED strips stuck to them.

![Lights staggered so they don't block each other](https://media.fisher.sh/blog/2021/11/23/rechargeable-glow-disc-box/13.jpg)

The lights were placed so that they charged the disc on the opposite side, and the strips didn't block each other.

![Wire cutting and soldering](https://media.fisher.sh/blog/2021/11/23/rechargeable-glow-disc-box/14.jpg)

Making these took the longest time, especially the wire-cutting and soldering...

![First test charge](https://media.fisher.sh/blog/2021/11/23/rechargeable-glow-disc-box/15.jpg)

This was my first test-charge.

![Yup. That worked.](https://media.fisher.sh/blog/2021/11/23/rechargeable-glow-disc-box/16.jpg)

Yup. That worked.

![Glow not perfectly even but super bright](https://media.fisher.sh/blog/2021/11/23/rechargeable-glow-disc-box/17.jpg)

It's not a perfectly even charge - but they're super bright. I hope that they get jostled while being carried, resulting in an even charge.

![First big 3D-printing project](https://media.fisher.sh/blog/2021/11/23/rechargeable-glow-disc-box/18.jpg)

I bought a 3D printer recently, and this was its first big project. Almost all the internals are 3D printed (plexiglass guides, disc holders, dimmer switch mounts, battery cage, etc.).
